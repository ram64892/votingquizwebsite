The Thanjavur Big Temple (Brihadeeswara Temple) is a famous temple dedicated to Shivan, built by Rajaraja Cholan in the 11th Century. The Shiva Linga in the main shrine is 8.7m in height, making it one of the largest in India. It also has a large shrine for Nandi facing the main shrine, and other smaller shrines for various other deities.
